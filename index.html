About Us

Welcome to EnergiX – a cutting-edge platform designed to provide smart energy insights and advanced forecasting for efficient energy management. Our project is the culmination of a dedicated effort by a team of passionate computer science students, blending state-of-the-art machine learning techniques with user-friendly web design to transform raw energy data into actionable intelligence.

At EnergiX, we believe that understanding and optimizing energy consumption is crucial for sustainability and cost efficiency. Our system leverages innovative forecasting models, including Facebook Prophet and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural networks, to deliver accurate daily and monthly predictions. These insights empower households, businesses, and institutions to make informed decisions, reduce waste, and manage energy more effectively.

Our multidisciplinary team combines expertise in data science, machine learning, and web development to build solutions that are both robust and scalable. We are committed to continuous improvement and welcome feedback to help us refine our platform and meet the evolving needs of energy management in today’s dynamic environment.
