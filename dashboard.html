Help & Instructions

How to Use the Dashboard

Uploading Data

1. Click "Upload CSV". Select your energy consumption file.

2. Enter additional details (Country, City, Type).

3. Choose a prediction model (Prophet or LSTM).

Viewing Archives

- Go to the "Archive" tab to view past analyses.

- Click "View CSV" to see your uploaded data.

- Click "View Analysis" to open energy insights.

Account Management

- Change username via "Account Center".

- Delete account permanently if needed.

Troubleshooting

Q: My additional insights are too short.

A: The system fetches data from the web. If insights are cut off, refresh the page or try again later.

Q: Archive graphs are missing.

A: Ensure you uploaded valid CSV files with correct datetime and energy consumption columns.
